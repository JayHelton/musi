CAGED System

Five movable major-chord shapes — C, A, G, E and D — map any major chord across the whole neck. Going up the fretboard the shapes always appear in the order C → A → G → E → D (then repeat), and neighbouring shapes share a root note, which is how they link together. The root notes (marked R) are your anchors: the C, A and D shapes have two roots; the G and E shapes have three. Shown in standard tuning.
